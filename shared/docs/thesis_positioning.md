# Thesis Positioning Statement

## Problem statement
Current monitoring of people with cognitive or functional vulnerability is often fragmented, reactive and highly dependent on caregiver observation alone.

## Proposed solution
CogniWatch integrates wearable-derived daily summaries, patient self-report, caregiver review, and optional digital cognitive-motor microassessments to detect changes over time and generate interpretable alerts.

## Non-diagnostic framing
The platform is intended for:
- monitoring,
- longitudinal follow-up,
- anomaly detection,
- caregiver support,
- and support for follow-up decisions.

It is **not** intended to provide clinical diagnosis.
